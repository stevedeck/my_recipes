Lemon Garlic Shrimp with Zucchini Noodles

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 medium zucchini, spiralized or julienned into noodles
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté for 1-2 minutes or until fragrant.
  2. Add the shrimp to the skillet and season with salt and black pepper. Cook for 2-3 minutes per side or until pink and cooked through.
  3. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  4. Add the zucchini noodles to the same skillet and sauté for 2-3 minutes or until tender-crisp.
  5. Add the cooked shrimp back to the skillet with the zucchini noodles. Squeeze the lemon juice over the top and toss everything together.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley. Enjoy!
