Teriyaki Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, sliced into thin strips
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced into thin strips
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced into thin strips
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, cornstarch, and sesame oil. Set aside.
  2. In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and season with salt and pepper. Cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
  3. Add the red and green bell peppers, onion, and garlic to the skillet. Cook for 3-5 minutes or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Add the chicken back to the pan with the vegetables. Pour the teriyaki sauce over the chicken and vegetables and stir to coat.
  5. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened and the chicken and vegetables are coated in sauce.
  6. Serve the stir-fry over cooked rice. Enjoy!
